How it works

1 Become a City Farmer

Create your account to get access to all the available crops. Get in touch with the farmers of the community and take a look on their farms and different investments.

2 Create your farm

Invest on a farm for yourself or share a farm with other farmers. The minimum investment is the 1% of the cost of the farm.

3 Monetize your farm

Once we harvest, we will distribute the production of the crop in proportional parts to the investment of each farmer. You can collect, send or sell your part as you wish.
